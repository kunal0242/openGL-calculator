To execute:
    go to the directory
    enter
    "make run1" in the terminal.

Function of the calculator:
    press "R" to rotate the calculator.
    press "-" to zoom out.
    press "+" to zoom in.
    press left click to show animation on the selected button, info about the button will be displayed in the console.
    press right click once to select which button to translate, once again press right click at the position where you want your button to translate.
    press arrow keys to translate the whole calculator accordingly.
    press "m" to reset.
    press esc to exit.
